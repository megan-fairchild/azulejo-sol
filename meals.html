Day 1 — First Night in Lisbon

🍲 Caldo Verde + Petiscos at a Local Tasca
Day 1 Dinner · May 16
▾
Where

Any tasca in Alfama or Chiado — ask Airbnb host for recs

Order
  • Caldo Verde (potato & kale soup)
  • Petiscos (Portuguese tapas)
  • Bread & olives (couvert)
Drink

Vinho Verde (young Portuguese white wine)

💡 Couvert (bread/olives/cheese at table) is NOT free — decline if you don't want it
Budget

~€20-30 per person

🥮 Pastéis de Belém
Day 2 Morning · May 17
▾
Where

Order

  • Dust with cinnamon + powdered sugar

Galão (Portuguese latte) or bica (espresso)

Day 6 — Carvoeiro

🐟 Cook at the Villa — Market-Fresh Fish

Dining Tips for Portugal

🍴 Good to Know

  • 💰 "Couvert" (bread/olives/cheese placed on your table) costs €2-5 — you can decline
  • 🕐 Lunch is the bigger meal in Portugal; dinner is lighter and later (8-9 PM)
  • ☕ "Uma bica, por favor" = an espresso, please
  • ☕ "Galão" = Portuguese latte
  • 💶 Tipping: 5-10% is generous; round up for casual meals
  • 🐟 Seafood is priced by weight at many restaurants — ask "Quanto custa?" before ordering
