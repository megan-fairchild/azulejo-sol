WARDROBE

What to pack for cobblestones, castles & beaches

Travel & Arrival Day 0–1 · May 15–16
▸
  • Comfortable travel outfit — leggings or soft pants, oversized tee or sweater, sneakers
  • Layer: light jacket or cardigan for the plane
  • Crossbody bag (hands-free for airport)
  • Compression socks for the long flight
  • Change of underwear in carry-on (just in case luggage is delayed!)
Belém & Alfama Day 2 · May 17
▸
  • Linen pants or midi skirt + cute blouse or flowy top
  • Comfortable walking shoes (COBBLESTONES — sneakers or low wedges)
  • Crossbody bag (pickpocket-safe in Alfama)
  • Sunglasses + sunscreen
  • Light cardigan for evening rooftop dinner
Sintra & Cabo da Roca Day 3 · May 18
▸
  • Athletic-chic: leggings or hiking pants + stylish tee or tank
  • Comfortable sneakers or light hiking shoes (Sintra hills are steep!)
  • Light jacket or windbreaker (Cabo da Roca is VERY windy and cooler)
  • Hair tie (wind at the cliffs!)
  • Small backpack for the day
Tile Workshop, Shopping & Fado Day 4 · May 19
▸
  • AM: Casual cute — jeans/pants + nice top (tile workshop = might get clay on you)
  • PM: Change for Fado dinner — smart-casual dress or blouse + nice pants
  • Comfortable shoes that can go from workshop to dinner
  • Scarf or wrap for the cool Alfama evening streets
  • Earrings/jewelry for Fado night out ✨
Drive to Odeceixe Day 5 · May 20
▸
  • Road trip casual — comfortable shorts or pants + tee
  • Swimsuit UNDER clothes (you might stop at Comporta beach!)
  • Sandals + sneakers in the car
  • Coverup for beach stops
Beach & Adventure Day 6 · May 21
▸
  • Swimsuit + coverup
  • Rashguard or swim top for SUP/surfing
  • Board shorts or swim leggings (optional)
  • Water shoes for rocky coves
  • Flip flops + sneakers (for coastal trail hike)
  • Sun hat, sunglasses, waterproof sunscreen
  • Light hoodie or flannel for evening stargazing
Return to Lisbon Day 7 · May 22
▸
  • Morning: Beach casual for last walk
  • Afternoon: Change to city outfit for farewell dinner
  • Farewell dinner look: your best outfit! Dress, jumpsuit, or cute top + pants
  • Comfortable shoes for one last Lisbon evening
Fly Home Day 8 · May 23
▸
  • Comfortable travel outfit (same as arrival or different combo)
  • Easy layers for temperature changes
